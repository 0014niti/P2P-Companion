## What is P2P Arbitrage?

Peer-to-Peer (P2P) arbitrage is the practice of buying a cryptocurrency on one platform where the price is low, and immediately selling it on another platform (or to another user) where the price is high. 

Unlike traditional spot trading, P2P prices are determined by individual merchants, local banking constraints, and regional fiat demand. This fragmentation creates massive opportunities.
